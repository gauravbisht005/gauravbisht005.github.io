We used to have our exams for almost 2 months (including internals and externals). The examinations have always been tricky. For most of the students, external factors have always had an impact on how much you'll be scoring rather than your actual knowledge in any particular field of subject; starting from your roll number (who's sitting in-front and behind you), be it your desk placed on the first of the row, the invigilator in-charge, the season in which the examination is being held, mood of the teacher checking your answer sheet based on those fellow classmates whose roll number used to come before you....
